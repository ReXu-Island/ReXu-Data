Description

Aims and Fit of Module(i.e. relationship to programme)
This module aims at cultivating students’ self-management abilities, helping them realize the importance of health and safety, form active learning habits. Besides, this module also enhances communicative abilities and teamwork spirits.
Learning Outcomes(for accreditation and other reasons,sub-headings could be added to this section)
Students completing the module successfully should be able to:
A.    Help students learn to manage their study and life, and can live or study independently.


B. Help students understand basic measures to protect themselves in face of dangerous situations.


C. Help students better arrange their time.


D. Give students general idea of autonomous learning, and take the initiative to study.


E. Raise their consciousness about physical and mental health.


F. Help students understand the communication principles and improve their interpersonal skills.


G. Help them understand cross-cultural differences.


H. Give students general idea on job seeking, career planning, and etc.


A. 教育学生如何在大学里学会自我管理，培养独立生活，学习的能力


B. 掌握安全知识，培养学生面对危机和灾难时的生存能力


C. 培养学生在大学合理安排时间的能力


D. 培养学生在学习上积极主动的能力，更多的自主学习，顺利完成学业


E. 树立身心健康意识，为完成学习任务和事业成功提供健康保障


F.了解沟通的概念和内涵，培养人际交往能力，逐步培养学生的表达力、表现力和表演力


G.认识跨文化沟通的重要性，掌握跨文化沟通方式及过程


H.引导同学结合工作环境，按照个人的特点选择相应的专业或职业，培养他们求职技巧和能力
