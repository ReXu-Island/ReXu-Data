Description

Aims and Fit of Module(i.e. relationship to programme)
To provide insights into the basic elements about digital image and video processing.



The first art of this module is focused on the fundamentals of visual signal representation and processing.



The second part will introduce the fundamentals of data, image, and video processing technologies.
Learning Outcomes(for accreditation and other reasons,sub-headings could be added to this section)
Students completing the module successfully should be able to:
A. Analyze the basic principles of digital image and video processing.


B. Analyze the techniques used in digital image and video processing, including image enhancement, image transformation, image compression and video coding, etc.


C. Evaluate of the effect of image and video processing.


D. Apply relevant image and video processing techniques to a given problem.
Method of Teaching and Learning
This module will be delivered through a combination of formal lectures and lab sessions. The lab experiments will be carried on using the MATLAB language and other software tools for image and video coding. For each lab, a brief report shall be prepared, which will be evaluated and will concur to determine the final grade. 

The examination will consist in a written exam with a few questions to be answered. 

The grade will be determined from the written exam and the evaluation of the reports of the computer labs.
Syllabus
1.	General introduction of this module, including the outline of the module contents, reasons about why this module is important, to help students have a good impression on this module.



  

2.	Image Enhancement, including the detailed techniques about the image enhancement algorithms. 



3.	Image Transform, including the general ideas of image transform, and some detailed techniques. 



4.         Introduction to data compression, including the concepts of compression, and some specific compression methods. 

  

5.	Morphological image processing, including the general idea of morphological operations, and some practical operations. 

6.	Multiresolution processing, including the concepts about the muti-resolution, and some representative methods. 

  

7.	Introduction to video processing, including basic concepts, and the relationship between image and video processing. 



8.	Revision- Summary of the module
