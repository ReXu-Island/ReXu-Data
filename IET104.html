Description

Aims and Fit of Module(i.e. relationship to programme)
The module requires the students to acquire the relevant knowledge, skills and experience on creation of movie and TV plays, and proficiently create and adapt movie and TV plays. It also aims to enhance the basic theoretical attainment and practical writing skills in the major of play adaption for the students, gradually cultivate the professional attainments of each student in movie and TV play adaption, and lay a foundation for development in movie and TV industry.
Learning Outcomes(for accreditation and other reasons,sub-headings could be added to this section)
Students completing the module successfully should be able to:
A	Identify the fundamental knowledge, laws and methods on writing of movie and TV plays, and managing the source materials, subjects and themes of movie and TV plays


B	Acquire the basic skills of movie and TV agencies, languages of movie and TV plays, and adapt movie and TV plays.


C	Identify transactions of intellectual property rights and plays, intrinsic relations between the movie and TV industrial layout, and operation of feature towns of a focal organisation.
Method of Teaching and Learning
Classroom teaching, lectures and creative practice.
Syllabus
1.	Basis of play writing


2.	Regulations on play writing


3.	Play structure


4.	Practice on play creation


5.	Interpretation and analysis of successful plays 


6.	Protection of intellectual rights and play transaction


7.	Creation of stage plays


8.	Interpretation of movie and TV culture industry
