Description

Aims and Fit of Module(i.e. relationship to programme)
To provide a comprehensive coverage and in-depth of the theory and design of digital communication systems at an level expressly for the fourth year undergraduate students.
Learning Outcomes(for accreditation and other reasons,sub-headings could be added to this section)
Students completing the module successfully should be able to:
A. Understand sources, effects and solutions for channel degradation, especially AWGN.

B. Understand digital baseband transmission and reception techniques.

C. Understand digital modulation and demodulation techniques.

D. Analyze signals of communication systems using math tools of probabilities and random process.

E. Analyze error probability of optimum receiver of a digital system.
Method of Teaching and Learning
This module will be delivered by a combination of formal lectures, problem classes, class demonstrations, and case studies.
